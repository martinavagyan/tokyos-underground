DISCOVER THE AUTHENTIC VIBE OF TOKYO

My name is Ryo Kido and I live in Tokyo’s vibrant Shinjuku district. Probably as yourself, I’m a passionate traveller and explorer. I lived in Europe and India before, and I travelled through Asia, Africa, and the Americas as well. My favorite part about Tokyo is that there hundreds of different neighborhoods, which have their own flair and thus attract different types of people. My aim is to show you the never-ending diversity that the city has to offer, which cannot be reflected solely through the tourist spots. Let us show you some real local neighborhoods and amazing street food.


Great to meet you, I’m Fumi Kawasaki.

I’m an eater, drinker, traveler, engineer, blogger, video host, and some other random things, but mostly my favorite thing is eating & drinking in local Izakaya with friends. I was born and raised in Tokyo. So I know many Izakayas in this city. We chose our favorite neighborhoods and authentic Izakayas for our tours. You would enjoy that. I was doing sumo for 10 years. So if you are interested we can talk about sumo or how I managed to loose a lot of weight after. Thank you for stopping by, and I look forward to connecting with you in our tours.
